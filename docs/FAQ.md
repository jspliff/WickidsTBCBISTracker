# FAQ

### Which phases are supported?

P1, P2, P3, P4, P5 for every class/spec.

### Does my list carry across characters?

BIS selections are **per character**. Settings (panel position, default phase, etc.) are **account-wide**.

### How does SoftRes integration work?

Paste a soft-reserve token string (from softres.it) into the import box. Items reserved by your raid members will highlight on your list. Export sends your own reservations back out as a token string.

### An item is missing from my list — bug?

Most likely it's a new item from a recent build, or a spec-specific item the addon hasn't classified yet. [Open an issue](https://github.com/jspliff/WickidsTBCBISTracker/issues) with the item name and which class/spec/phase you expected it in.

### Can I use a custom BIS list?

Not in the current release. Custom-list support is a planned feature — track progress on the [issues page](https://github.com/jspliff/WickidsTBCBISTracker/issues).

### Is this for Era / Season of Discovery / Retail?

No — TBC Classic only (Interface 20505). SoD and Retail have their own ecosystems.
